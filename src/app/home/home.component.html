About this Dashboard

This dashboard is designed to provide employees with quick access to important information and tools for managing their work-related activities.

Key Features:

  • My Details: View personal information and details specific to your employee profile.
  • Leave:
  • Leave Details: Track your leave balance, including Casual Leave and Medical Leave.
  • Leave Report: Review detailed leave records by month, with information about leave type, duration, and reasons.
  • Service Request: You can view the ticket id.
  • Desktop Support: You can view the ticket id.

Navigate through the menu on the left to access these features and manage your details effectively.

ChainBot Features

ChainBot is designed to assist employees with various services efficiently. Below are the key features available:

  • Leave Application: Submit your leave requests directly through ChainBot. You can specify the type of leave, dates, reasons, and get real-time updates on your application status.
  • Service Request: Use ChainBot to submit a request for any internal services, such as report Bug, Change Request, Support Request..
  • Desktop Support: Raise a desktop support request for IT assistance through ChainBot.
